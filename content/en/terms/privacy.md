We process personal data on the following bases, as applicable:

## 6. Your rights (LGPD – Article 18)

You have the right to:

- **Confirmation** of the existence of data processing.
- **Access** to your data.
- **Correction** of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
- **Anonymization, blocking, or deletion** of unnecessary or unlawfully processed data.
- **Portability** of data to another service provider, when applicable.
- **Deletion** of data processed on the basis of consent.
- **Information** about with whom we share data.
- **Information** about the possibility of not consenting and the consequences.
- **Revocation** of consent.

To exercise any of these rights, send an email to **contato@youbetschool.com** with the subject “LGPD – Exercise of rights” and your identification. We will respond within a timeframe compatible with the LGPD.

## 9. Cookies and similar technologies

### What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit the website. They help us remember preferences, measure site usage, and improve your experience, always in compliance with this policy and the LGPD.

### How we use cookies

- **Essential:** necessary for the website to function (e.g., language preferences).
- **Analytics:** to understand how the site is used (e.g., Google Analytics), in aggregate form, to improve content and navigation.
- **Preferences:** to remember settings you have chosen.

### Third-party cookies

We may use third-party tools (e.g., Google Analytics) that set their own cookies. Use of such data is governed by those third parties’ privacy policies. You can configure your browser to refuse or limit cookies; in some cases, this may affect website functionality.

### Managing cookies

You can adjust cookie settings in your browser (Help or Settings menu). Consent for marketing/analytics cookies may also be managed through a consent tool displayed on the website, when available.
