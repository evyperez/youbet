## Play soccer abroad

YouBet! School offers the best exchange for those who love playing soccer. With our program, you train in the United States, improve your English through ESL – English as a Second Language – and compete with multicultural teams, visit Major League Soccer clubs, enjoy theme parks, skiing, and main cultural and tourist attractions.

The program lasts two weeks and all classes are taught by American teachers, deepening your knowledge and cultural experience.
